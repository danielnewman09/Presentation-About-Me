Thoughts from Post-Grad

  • Don't expect to get things right the first time around
  • If you know you want to pursue grad school, reach out to a professor or grad students you know
  • Don't spend too much time comparing yourself to your peers. Just do the best you can
  • Learn to write good code
    • Pyimagesearch.com
    • Corey Schafer
  • Learn how to use Git/Github
